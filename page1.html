About Us


Northside Locksmiths is a privately owned Queensland company providing locksmithing and associated security products and services to the retail, domestic, commercial, and automotive markets.

The supply, installation, repair and servicing of locks and keys is undertaken for these markets. Our Fleet of Service Vans are available for on-site or emergency work. We also offer a comprehensive range of security locks, automotive locking systems, transponder keys, garage remotes and new and second hand safes from our head office at 16 Boothby street Kedron 4031.

N A Moses Holdings started trading in 1981. Since commencement, we have established a strong reputation for both quality products and customer service at reasonable prices. .

Our Apprentices have twice been awarded the prestigious Master Locksmiths Association of Australasia, Apprentice of The Year Award and the Company has won recognition from the MLAA Queensland branch for our services in assisting in the establishment of the Queensland apprentice training.

Since 1981, N A Moses Holdings has incorporated the following companies - the dates of commencement are detailed as follows:

Northside Locksmiths - 19th August 1988
Queensland Safe Co. - 8th May 1989
Ashgrove Locksmiths - 4th January 1991
Ascot Locksmiths - 14th October 1993
Keperra Locksmiths - lst July 1994
Redcliffe Locksmiths - 5th November 2001
Moreton Bay Locksmiths - 13th September 2007

All our companies offer the same fast and efficient mobile locksmithing service and comprehensive range of products and services from our head office at 16 Boothby Street Kedron. Operational procedures are consistent for each Company. Trained staff are available to offer expert advice in all matters relating to Locksmithing and security products.

As a member of the Master Locksmiths Association of Australasia (MLAA), this company is bound to and abides by the Code of Ethics, trade customs and practices set down by the Master Locksmiths Association of Australasia.

MLAA Code of Ethics - It shall be the duty of every staff member, by virtue of membership of the Association, to accept and adhere to this "Code of Ethics":
- To place the principles of trust and integrity above all temptation. 
- To maintain a policy of efficient and honest service, adhering to the highest moral values.
- To act with the utmost prudence in safeguarding confidential knowledge entrusted to him.
- To carry out the responsibility of maintaining the regulations and other ordinances pertaining to his association.
